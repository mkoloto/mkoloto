AH Team

Herbert Rwamibazi

Managing Director.

AH Consulting

Herbert is the Managing Director of AH Consulting, a role he took since 2004. He has over 20 years of experience in consulting work across the African continent, leading the delivery of results to clients in different organisations in varied service lines. Herbert is responsible for the overall strategic direction and delivery of the AH Consulting vision.

Osbert Atusaasiire – Bsc. Civil Eng.

Director Business Development

Director of Operations Engineering Services/Business Development

Osbert is a Director of Operations at AH Consulting responsible for both Engineering Services and Business Development. He has over 10 years’ experience and has been at the responsible for the establishment and growth of the engineering services in the firm.

Cuthbert Kagabo – MBA (Oil and Gas), B.Com, ACCA, CISA

Dupty Managing Director

Deputy Managing Director

Cuthbert is the Deputy Managing Director of AH Consulting, a role he took since December 2011. He has over 10 years’ experience in consulting and is responsible for the overall delivery operational targets. Cuthbert previously worked with PricewaterhouseCoopers’ Assurance and Business Advisory Services where he gained experience in various sectors of the economy. Cuthbert has experience in working on projects in Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, Sudan, Kenya, Chad, Ethiopia, UK, Ghana and South Africa.

Edgart Katarahweire BSc. Ed, CCNA, CISA, CISM.CEO TSS
