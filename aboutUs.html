About Us

Histroy

More than 10 years in Consulting

In 2014, AH Consulting will make 10 years in consulting business. With clients in all sectors of the economy across the African continent, it has been a road of pursuit of excellence. From a humble beginning of a handful of staff, AH Consulting has a staff compliment supplemented by local, regional and international associates delivering client results. Working with world class partners, we have been pioneers in delivery of world class solutions and services

Difference

At the forefront of client delivery

Determined thought processes combined with consulting expertise and experience are the foundations for consistent delivery of client success. We have a passion to succeed through delivering clients services and solutions with excellence. Our difference lies in many years of contributing to the changes our clients experience from our work.

Offices

At Your Service

Our corporate headquarters are in Kampala, Uganda from where we serve all clients across the African continent. We have branches in Rwanda, Ethiopia, Kenya, Mozambique, Cote d’Iivoire, Malawi, Swaziland and South Africa. In countries where we don’t have operations, we establish country based project offices as and when it is necessary either independently or working with partners.
